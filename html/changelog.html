05 October 2018

PsyCommando updated:

  • Added a supply pack crate of 10 Duct Tape rolls. Rejoice!
  • Made duct tape rolls have a finite number of usages. 30 to be exact.
  • Crafting, surgery, restraints, and repairs involving tape all require a certain number of uses from a tape roll now.

30 September 2018

Kurgis updated:

  • NT Central Command redefined as CA Relay Outpost in map defines, should affect automated announcements and emergency messages.

Nappist updated:

  • Makes windoors deconstructable.

PsyCommando updated:

  • Fixed pressure regulators being unusable in space, or in an unpowered area, after reloading.

26 September 2018

Kurgis updated:

  • Added Bridge Secretary to the job orders in Operations, allowing access to a command headset.
  • Certain crates & lockers ordered from supply will no longer be nameless.
  • Access requirements on supply packs have been correctly set on all orders with secure crates or lockers. Orders which includes a secure locker or crate should no longer spawn with 'null' access, preventing anyone from opening it.
  • Removed entertainment headsets from the Clown, Mime, and Journalist supply orders. Removed guns from all operations supply orders.
  • Removed musician supply order from Operations, as it lacked proper content. Moved music devices to a seperate supply order.
  • Adjusted the cost & contents of various supply pack orders.
  • The Head of Security crate now requires leader access, all other head of staff job orders including warden now require command access to open.

Nappist updated:

  • Removed the grid_check aka power outage event from procing.

ingles98 updated:

  • State Of Matter Pt.2 - Unifies reagents and gases, condensing/evaporation system.
  • Area Reagent Outlet - APC concept - Pumps reagents to whatever object requests it. Can be connected to a pipe network.
  • Big changes to puddles, including fixes and real evaporation mechanism. Puddles should be comprehended as general liquid mechanism. (Gas counter-part)
  • Big changes to the drains. Deprecated drains will automatically destroy on load, refunding 2 steel sheets. New drains drain nearby liquids (puddles) into a pipe network.
  • Changes Showers and Sinks in order to request water from the ARO. No longer pumps infinite water.
  • Pipe-dispenser now has ARO and Drain available to be dispensed.
  • Removes drains (deprecated) from recipe list on Steel.
  • Reagents and Gases now have a base boiling point (At one atmosphere) (TODO: Set boiling points to the rest of the gases and reagents)
  • Canisters, uvents and dp vents will check for gas's boiling point before outputting them as a gas.
  • Lowers spilling amount and rate of reagents when transferring from a container to another.

rhicora updated:

  • Made turrets treat cyborgs with same threat criteria as humans

24 September 2018

Kurgis updated:

  • Changed all references to Tajaran to alien or xeno.
  • Killed innocent Tajarans hiding in the code.
