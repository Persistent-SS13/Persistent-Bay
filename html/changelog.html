  • Created new cargo tabs, along with various new orderables. This being flooring, custodial, livecargo, non-essentials, and assignments.

  • Removed empty regeant cartridge order as you can craft them.
  • Removed various improper tags and labels from cargo which didn't need them.
  • Removed mention of the word crate or order from cargo orders, as it's already implied they will come in such a container.
  • Adjusts the price of job crates, after moving their gear into other orders.
  • Re-arranged cargo contents into various tabs, operations now focuses on the actual operation of Cargo. Job equipment/clothes have been moved into the new assignments tab. Various orders split between non-essentials and hospitality, with hospitality focusing on the usual duties of a Kitchen and Bar.

  • You may now order a fax machine through cargo, usage result may vary.
  • NT Central Command redefined as CA Relay Outpost in map defines, should affect automated announcements and emergency messages.

ourpar updated:

  • Hand-rolled cigarettes are once again craftable. Requires 1x carboard in the handcrafting menu.
  • The 'smoking pipe' and the 'corn cob pipe' are now obtainable, respectively found in 'Clothing - Luxury accessories' and as a wooden crafting recipe.

  • Printing a single cable piece from the autolathe no longer prints a whole stack
  • Suit cycler units now use core accesses
  • Proximity sensor no longer requires 'waste' material in the autolathe

  • Welding backpack tank and painted welding masks are now orderable from cargo, under engineering tab.
  • Welding tools printed from the autolathe no longer include fuel.
  • Adjusted item size and production price of experimental welding tool.
  • Suit jackets now have item sprites, allowing them to be safely worn without them getting stuck on your suit slot.
  • Added mob sprites for 'warden heavy armored vest' and 'commander heavy armored vest'.

  • Extinguishers printed from the autolathe now start with 0 water
  • Added Mining, Engineering, Medical, Science, and Security softsuits into cargo with their own sprites. Rejoice poor people!
  • All spacesuits now come in large crates, because consistency.
  • Civilian/generic softsuits and emergency softsuits have new sprites.
